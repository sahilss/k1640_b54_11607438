Sahil sharma, Founder, Road captain & Travel Advisor

Motorbike touring is my favorite adventure activity & my favorite place is Himalaya to ride motorcycle. I am so passionate about motorcycles & ride that inspired me to establish a Motorcycle tour company. I have worked in a travel agency for 3 years that give me firsthand knowledge about travel industry & my adventure skills on motorcycle give me idea to operate motorcycle tours in different conditions. Today I spend my most of the time on Motorcycle to guide riders in Himalaya, South India, and Rajasthan & when I am not on the tour, I spend time to make best tour itineraries for our riders & give reply to queries regarding Motorcycle tours in India. I love my job & riding legendary Royal Enfield. As an owner I take everything personally & monitor all functions like hotel bookings, logistics, transport, motorcycles & staff appointments so that we together can give best services to our clients.
